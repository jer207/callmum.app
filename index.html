Call Mum

An app for those who care

I struggle to stay in touch with those I care about. It's not lack of caring, we're not estranged, and I'm not physically blocked. For whatever reason, I am susceptible to my attention being swept up by what's right in front of me.

Recurring meetings on my calendar, to do lists, haven't worked. The timing of alerts was always off. So I am building this app to remind me to call my mother. This app will notify me when it matters.
